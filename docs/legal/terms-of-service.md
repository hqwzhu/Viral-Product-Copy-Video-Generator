ENHE Product Promo Maker (formerly ENHE Promotion Manager) provides a browser extension, local Codex workflow commands, and optional ENHE-hosted promotion task execution.

- Use only product URLs and materials you have rights to process.
- Review generated content before publishing.

- Do not use the service to bypass login, captcha, account verification, platform review, or risk-control systems.

The service creates publish packages and workflow outputs. It does not guarantee platform acceptance, virality, traffic, sales, revenue, or search ranking. Manual publish packages are the default. Official API publishing requires separate user authorization and explicit approval.
